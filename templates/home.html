Immunization Project

This app shows the success of programs using the measles vaccine in different countries, across different years. It is based on four related datasets from PROVIDE SOURCE HERE, described below.

NOTE: Given the large amount of data presented, this app best viewed on a desktop computer with a large monitor, and a browser expanded to occupy most or all of the screen.

Datasets to explore:

  • Deaths - deaths caused by vaccine-preventable diseases, varied by country and year. (Original dataset includes other diseases, but here limited to measles.)
  • Coverage - measles vaccine coverage varied by country and time. Also includes total population of each country as a demographic comparison.
  • Children - share of young children (12-23 months) vaccinated by country and year.
  • Disagrees - 2015 evaluation of share of population that disagrees vaccines are safe. Varied by country.
